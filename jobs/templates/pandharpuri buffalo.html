Pandharpuri Buffalo

The Pandharpuri is a breed of water buffalo native to the dry regions of Solapur, Kolhapur, Satara and Sangli in India. The name is derived from the town Pandharpur in Solapur. The Pandharpuri buffalo has 45-50 centimetre-long horns which are sometimes twisted. Lactation length is 350 days, calving interval is 465 days. Milk yield is 1400 kg/305 days.
